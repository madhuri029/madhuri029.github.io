2.Smart Traffic And Emergency Alert System

➡️A Smart Traffic and Emergency Alert System works by using sensors, cameras, and GPS to continuously monitor road traffic conditions.

➡️When an emergency vehicle like an ambulance or fire truck is detected, the system automatically changes traffic signals to give it a clear path and sends alerts to nearby vehicles and control centers.

➡️This helps reduce traffic jams, ensures faster emergency response, minimizes accidents, and improves overall road safety and traffic efficiency.

➡️The system uses real-time data analysis to predict congestion and adjust signal timings dynamically, improving traffic flow even during peak hours.

➡️It enables centralized monitoring, allowing traffic authorities to track emergencies, respond quickly, and make better decisions using live traffic insights.
